**Authors:** <strong>Zibo Hu</strong>, Zhe Lin, Renjing Hou, Xingyu Qin, Jianwang Zhai, and Kang Zhao.

HLS-Timer targets early-stage timing estimation for High-Level Synthesis. It models HLS designs with graph-based representations and predicts fine-grained path-level delay, providing timing guidance before expensive downstream implementation flows.
